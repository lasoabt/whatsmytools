About QR Codes

QR (Quick Response) codes are two-dimensional barcodes that can store various types of information including URLs, text, contact information, and more. They can be easily scanned by smartphones and dedicated QR code readers.

Common Use Cases

  • Website URLs and social media links
  • Contact information (vCard format)
  • WiFi network credentials
  • Email addresses and phone numbers
  • Event details and calendar entries
  • Product information and tracking

Error Correction Levels

  • Low (L): ~7% damage recovery
  • Medium (M): ~15% damage recovery
  • Quartile (Q): ~25% damage recovery
  • High (H): ~30% damage recovery

Higher error correction allows the QR code to be read even if parts are damaged or obscured, but makes the code more complex.
