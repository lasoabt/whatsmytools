PRIVACY.POLICY

PRIVACY.PROTECTION.PROTOCOL :: CLIENT.SIDE.PROCESSING :: NO.DATA.TRANSMISSION

Last Updated: December 19, 2024

EXECUTIVE.SUMMARY

WhatsMyTools.com is committed to protecting your privacy. Our tools process data entirely on your device (client-side) and we do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers.

1. INFORMATION.WE.COLLECT

1.1 Personal Data

We do NOT collect any personal data through our tools. All processing happens locally in your browser.

1.2 Technical Data

We may automatically collect limited technical information:

  • IP address (for basic analytics)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website

1.3 Cookies

We use minimal cookies only for:

  • Essential website functionality
  • Anonymous usage analytics (if implemented)

No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

1.4 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand website usage:

  • Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data
  • IP addresses are anonymized
  • Data is used solely for improving the website
  • You can opt-out using browser settings or extensions
  • View Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

1.5 Google AdSense

We display advertisements through Google AdSense:

  • Google may use cookies to serve relevant ads
  • Ad personalization can be controlled through Google settings
  • Visit Google Ad Settings to manage preferences
  • Ads help keep our tools free for everyone

2. HOW.WE.USE.INFORMATION

Any technical data collected is used solely for:

  • Website performance monitoring
  • Security protection
  • Understanding general usage patterns
  • Technical improvements

3. CLIENT.SIDE.PROCESSING

All our tools process data directly in your browser:

  • No data is sent to our servers for processing
  • Your files, text, and inputs remain on your device
  • Tools work offline once loaded
  • We cannot see or access your data

4. THIRD.PARTY.SERVICES

Some tools may use external APIs for functionality:

  • IP Detection: ipify.org and ip-api.com for IP address lookup
  • QR Generation: api.qrserver.com for QR code images
  • Google Fonts: For typography (fonts.googleapis.com)

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

5. YOUR.RIGHTS.UNDER.GDPR

Since we don't collect personal data, GDPR rights are largely not applicable. However, you have:

  • Right to Information: This privacy policy
  • Right to Object: Disable cookies in your browser
  • Right to Erasure: Clear browser data
  • Right to Portability: Not applicable (no data stored)

6. DATA.SECURITY

We implement security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • No server-side data storage
  • Regular security updates
  • Secure hosting infrastructure

7. INTERNATIONAL.TRANSFERS

Since processing occurs client-side, no data transfers occur. Technical data may be processed in the EU/US where our hosting provider operates.

8. DATA.RETENTION

We do not store personal data. Technical logs may be retained for up to 30 days for security and performance purposes.

9. CHILDREN.PRIVACY

Our services do not target children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

10. CHANGES.TO.POLICY

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use indicates acceptance.

11. CONTACT.INFORMATION

For privacy questions or concerns:

  • Website: WhatsMyTools.com
  • Data Controller: WhatsMyTools.com
  • Legal Basis: Legitimate interest for technical functionality

PRIVACY.GUARANTEE

WE GUARANTEE: Your data never leaves your device when using our tools. All processing is performed locally in your browser for maximum privacy protection.
